Write A Fun Product Description For Your Website, Ebay Or Etsy











About:

Make your products shine with professionally written descriptions. I have been writing product descriptions for various websites for over 12 years. Well-written descriptions will highlight the benefits of your products while enticing your customers to purchase.



I will review your product images, current descriptions (if applicable) and the key features that you would like to highlight. Then, I will write a fun, informative description that you can use on your website, catalog or any of your marketing materials. All product descriptions will be appropriate for your own store's websites and third party selling sites like eBay, ETSY, Amazon and more.

 

I will send you a finished document in Word format. I offer several packages with gig extras to ensure that you receive everything you need for your product listing. Please contact me with any questions.



Each listing includes:

1 Product Title

5 Bullet Points (approximately 100 characters each)

1 Description (approximately 150 words)
